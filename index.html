About Tone Bo$$

Tone Bo$$ is a versatile Caribbean artist who delivers powerful lyrics. From hard-hitting bangers to emotional tracks, his music takes listeners on a journey through rhythm, passion, and storytelling, making every track a unique experience for fans of authentic, high-quality music. Hailing from St Lucia, Tone Bo$$’s life journey is what motivates and inspires him to share his story and talent with the world.

Music

Released 7 singles, 2 feature singles (Gwadeloupe Cho, Stepping Too Hard), and 1 album (RedDot, 14 tracks, incl. 2 features). Collaborated with artists like Brasco, La Rose, Kevin, Mata LS, Green Money, and producers Blaze on the Bords, Stunner on da Beat, Mr-Tod the Riddim God, and Gringo’z Records.
